How Our Platform Works

A seamless blend of human expertise and artificial intelligence.

1. Symptom Input & AI Analysis

You begin by entering your symptoms into our AI Prediction Model. The system analyzes this data against a vast medical database to identify potential conditions and their likelihood.

2. Engage with the AI Assistant

Our Gemini-powered AI Health Assistant helps you understand the preliminary results. You can ask follow-up questions, get definitions for complex terms, and receive guidance on the next steps in a clear, conversational manner.

3. Connect with a Specialist

Based on the AI's analysis, our platform recommends the most appropriate specialist for your condition. You can book a secure video consultation directly through our system for a definitive diagnosis and treatment plan.

4. Personalized Care & Follow-up

Your consultation details, prescriptions, and care plan are securely stored in your personal dashboard. Our system provides follow-up reminders and makes it easy to reconnect with your doctor or the AI assistant.
