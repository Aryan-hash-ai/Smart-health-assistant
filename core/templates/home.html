Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

Is my personal health information secure?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and use bank-level, end-to-end encryption for all communication and data storage. Our platform is fully HIPAA compliant, ensuring your information is always protected.

How do I pay for a consultation?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and several digital wallets. Payment is processed securely through our platform after your consultation is confirmed. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Can I get a prescription through HealthFast?

Yes, our licensed doctors can issue prescriptions when medically necessary. You can choose to have the prescription sent electronically to your local pharmacy or use our integrated online pharmacy for home delivery.

What if I need to see a specialist not listed?

Our network of specialists is constantly growing. If you need a specialty that is not currently listed, please contact our support team. We can often arrange a consultation with a relevant expert through our extended network.
